# /kg-challenge — Devil's Advocate

Argue against a stated claim (or pick the most central one from the wiki) using only evidence from the knowledge base.

**Flow:**
1. If no claim given, pick highest-confidence INFERRED edge or most central concept
2. Find all evidence that supports the claim
3. Find all evidence that contradicts or weakens it
4. Present the counter-argument with citations
5. Ask: "Does this change anything, or does the original claim still hold?"

This is not about being right — it's about stress-testing knowledge.
